# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Agents and administrators of businesses using the WhatsBi WhatsApp CRM. They use the tool in a professional, task-focused workflow, often managing customer conversations in real-time, responding to orders, updating lead stages, and analyzing team performance.

## Product Purpose
WhatsBi is a WhatsApp CRM designed to help businesses manage customer communication, orders, services, and agent assignments efficiently. The system provides real-time chat, template message sending, order tracking, and performance analytics to increase customer conversions.

## Brand Personality
- Professional
- Streamlined
- Trustworthy

## Anti-references
- SaaS-cream minimalism (overly warm beige/cream tones, ghost cards with thin grey lines and massive soft shadows).
- Hyper-playful animations (bouncy, elastic effects, cluttered floaters).
- Slow, blocking load states.

## Design Principles
1. **Speed & Efficiency**: Actions must feel instantaneous. Optimistic UI updates and efficient polling ensure agents stay in the flow.
2. **Clear Information Architecture**: Clear visual division between customer lists, active chats, details, and analytics. Screen space is optimized for high information density without visual noise.
3. **Task-Focused Micro-interactions**: Animations serve to guide focus, communicate state shifts (e.g. active transitions, open/close dropdowns, loading spinner transitions), and verify action success rather than to decorate.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of body copy must meet WCAG AA (>= 4.5:1).
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` media queries across all style sheets.
- Maintain accessible keyboard focus states and logical interactive element naming.
